description: "A comprehensive guide to getting started with Urbit"
---

Getting started with Urbit involves several key steps: acquiring an Urbit ID, setting up your Urbit OS instance, and connecting to the network. This guide will walk you through each stage of the process, whether you choose to self-host or use a hosting provider.

Urbit is designed to be accessible to both technical users who want full control and everyday users who prefer a managed solution. The sections that follow will help you understand your options and get your ship running.
